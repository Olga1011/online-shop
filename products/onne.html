Clarifying Cleanser 50ml

Calm inflammation and moisturise with Onne’s Clarifying Cleanser. Specially formulated for broken, sensitive and irritated skin, this gentle face cleanser works to clean, clarify and correct the skin. An antioxidant, antiseptic powerhouse, Chamomile works to protect the skin, while tackling blemishes. Calendula and White Oak Bark work together to reduce inflammation and calm skin irritations, while moisture-enhancing Rose Water reduces redness to nourish and hydrate the skin. This deeply restorative cleanser works well for all skin types. Hydrating and astringent, this facial cleanser is perfect for challenging skin conditions such as eczema, rosacea and acne.
