Products description

Organic purifying facial cleanser 60ml

Dr. Alkaitis' non-drying organic face cleanser gently but thoroughly melts away impurities, leaving your skin dewy, soft and radiant. Strengthens your skin and aims to balance and restore your skin's natural pH-balance. Recommended for all skin complexions. Contains Vitamin A from organic Carrot and Marigold Extract and Vitamin C from Camu Camu and Acerola extracts.

DIRECTIONS

Use 1-2 times per week. Apply a generous layer to clean, dry skin. Avoid the eyes and mouth. Leave for 15-20 minutes then gently massage the masque to melt jojoba esters. Rinse off with warm water and follow with recommended moisturiser. Use spatula to dispense product. Store below 30°C. To ensure skin compatibility, patch test first. If irritation occurs, discontinue use and seek medical advice

INGREDIENTS

Into Dr. Alkaitis' Castile Cleansing Base (compounded from virgin, cold pressed olive*, shea*, jojoba*, coconut*, sesame*, castor* and borage* oils), the following ingredients are added: aloe vera gel*, Dr. Alkaitis' Herbal Skin Repair Complex (made up of chamomile*, plantain*, black elder*, greater nettle*, myrrhº, bitter orange*, rosemary*, marsh mallow*, benzoinº, marigold*, true lavender*, sea wrackº and atlantic seaweedº), vegetable glycerin*, citrus seed extract*, natural vitamin A and C complex, natural full spectrum vitamin E complex (D-alpha, beta, gamma, delta tocopherols) and a blend of 100% pure essential oils.
